@Brick

	Prevalent (Default) (Transparently persisted. All calls to any prevalent brick are synchronized with all other calls to all other prevalent bricks)

	@NonPrevalent (Not persisted by the container. "Brick-Managed Persistence")
		@Concurrent (Calls are free-threaded, not synchronized)
		Single Threaded (Default) (All calls are synchronized on the brick, including callbacks)
			@Gui (Automatically called in AWT/Swing GUI thread)
